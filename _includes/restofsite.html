A PHP user group for the Dundee and Angus area

We meet towards the end of every month and feature a guest speaker covering PHP related topics as well as topics of note from the wider software development community.

Anyone interested in networking with like-minded people, whether new to the world of PHP development or a seasoned professional is welcome to attend and speak.

Drinks, food and a projector are normally available.
